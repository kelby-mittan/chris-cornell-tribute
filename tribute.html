I Am the Highway: A Tribute to Chris Cornell

Members of Soundgarden, Temple of the Dog, and Audioslave, as well as Foo Fighters, Metallica, Josh Homme, Fiona Apple, and Brandi Carlile were all on hand to pay tribute to Chris Cornell as part of an all-star tribute concert held at The Forum in Los Angeles on January 16, 2019 . Organized by Cornell's wife, Vicky, "I Am the Highway: A Tribute to Chris Cornell" featured 42 distinct performances and a multitude of surprises guests, making for an unforgettable evening spanning nearly five hours in length.
